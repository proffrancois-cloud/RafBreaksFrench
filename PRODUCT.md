# Product

## Register

product

## Users

DP French B SL teachers and students using FrenchEase as a daily exam-preparation workspace. They need to move quickly between theme, topic, culture, Paper 1, Paper 2 reading, Paper 2 listening, IA practice, questions, audio, and markschemes without relearning the interface.

## Product Purpose

FrenchEase helps learners prepare for IB-style French B SL tasks with generated, original practice material organized by DP theme and topic. Success means a user can find the right practice item, understand the immediate task, work through it, and review feedback with minimal interface friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, institutional, precise. The product should feel closer to a professional education platform than a generic landing-page template.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS starter pages, oversized marketing heroes, decorative card grids, rainbow color use, purple-blue gradient styling, vague motivational copy, and any layout that looks assembled from a default template rather than designed around DP exam practice.

## Design Principles

- Put the current learning task first; keep reference material available through progressive disclosure.
- Use recognition over recall: visible theme, topic, paper, selected text/audio, and next action should always be clear.
- Let the Francophonie palette provide identity and wayfinding, not decoration.
- Keep density professional: compact enough for teachers, calm enough for students.
- Favor consistent controls, focus states, and language over novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive controls. Do not rely on color alone to communicate meaning. Maintain visible focus states, keyboard-reachable controls, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts for laptop and mobile use.
